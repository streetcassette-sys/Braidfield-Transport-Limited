Pre-Dispatch Policy

A pre-dispatch form must be generated and signed by both the Clearing Agent and Driver before a truck's status can be updated to "Crossed Border" or "Dispatched" at any border post. This captures: ETA to next destination, road toll balance, council receipt, cash paid for trip, and vehicle status.
