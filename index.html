Our Documented Process

9-Step Cross-Border Logistics Flow

Every Braidfield shipment follows this exact process — from document collection to final offloading at the Congo or Tanzania destination. All steps are tracked in real time.

Phase 1 — Documentation & Dispatch
1
PREP
Documents Received
Client provides all required documentation:
Commercial Invoice
Delivery Note
Customs T1 Document
Movement Sheet
2
PREP
Cargo Loaded & Dispatched
Cargo loaded and released from depot to Exit border. All items verified against delivery note and invoice before sealing.
3
BORDER
Exit Border Clearance
Exit border agent acquits all documents and officially releases cargo to Zambia. This triggers the ZRA registration process.
Phase 2 — ZRA Registration & T1 Issuance
4
ZRA
ZRA Ascuyda Entry
Braidfield Transport creates the entry in the ZRA Ascuyda system. This registers the shipment formally with Zambia Revenue Authority and generates a system reference number.
5
ZRA
Payment Made
Payment made for entry into the ZRA Ascuyda system. Payment reference number recorded and payment receipt attached to the trip file.
6
ZRA
T1 & Release Order Generated
Release order generated → T1 document issued for 5-day transit through Zambia.
⏱ 5-day T1 transit timer starts NOW
Phase 3 — Cross-Border Transit & Final Delivery
7
TRANSIT
Crossed to Congo
At Sakania (Zambia side): cargo is officially acquitted. Trucks then cross to Sakania Congo side — entering the final leg of the journey.
8
CONGO
Congo Final Destination
Buyer declares to Congolese customs → TR8 document generated. Buyer must pay taxes for T1 clearance.
48h parking limit — $25/day fine after
9
COMPLETE
Offloaded at Destination
T1 finalised → truck proceeds to offloading point. Cargo is received, signed off, and the trip is officially closed in the system.
